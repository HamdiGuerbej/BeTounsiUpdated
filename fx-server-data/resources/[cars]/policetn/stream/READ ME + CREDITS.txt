_CREATING MOD FOLDER _

1-Ta3mel  dossier esmou mods fi el game directory 

_ temchi fi C:\Program Files\Rockstar Games\Grand Theft Auto V\update\x64\dlcpacks ta3mel copier li patchday3ng , patchday2ng, patchday4ng 
ta3mlelhom coller fi C:\Program Files\Rockstar Games\Grand Theft Auto V\mods\update\x64\dlcpacks

_temchi fi C:\Program Files\Rockstar Games\Grand Theft Auto V\update copier li update.rpl
ta3mellou coller fi C:\Program Files\Rockstar Games\Grand Theft Auto V\mods\update

_temchi fi C:\Program Files\Rockstar Games\Grand Theft Auto V copier x64e.rpf
ta3mel coller fi C:\Program Files\Rockstar Games\Grand Theft Auto V\mods
(tnajem tsob ay vehicle mod w t7othom houni mouch ken el mod hedha :D)
